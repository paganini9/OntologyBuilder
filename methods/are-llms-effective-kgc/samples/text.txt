A Product consists of several Parts that engineers design and assemble.
Each Pump moves coolant through the cooling channels of the engine.
A CoolantPump is installed near the Radiator to keep temperatures stable.
A Motor drives the mechanical Assembly during normal operation.
An ElectricMotor uses a Battery and a Controller to power the wheels.
The Controller controls the Motor and monitors the Battery state.
Engineers test every Assembly before the Product ships to customers.
