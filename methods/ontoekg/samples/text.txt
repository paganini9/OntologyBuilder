The Pump moves coolant through the cooling loop of the assembly line.
An ElectricMotor drives the Pump that circulates the fluid.
The CoolantPump supplies coolant to the ElectricMotor during operation.
A TemperatureSensor monitors the ElectricMotor and reports overheating.
The ControlUnit manages the TemperatureSensor and the Pump together.
Each Motor powers a Component on the production line.
