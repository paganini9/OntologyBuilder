An ElectricMotor is a type of Motor that converts electrical energy into rotation.
The CoolantPump is a Pump that circulates fluid to keep the engine cool.
Every Motor and every Pump is a mechanical Component of the Product.
A TemperatureSensor is a Sensor that reports the operating Temperature.
The ControlUnit reads each Sensor and drives the ElectricMotor.
A HydraulicPump and an ElectricMotor are mounted on the same Assembly.
