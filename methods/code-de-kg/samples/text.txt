The Engine drives the Pump and the Controller monitors the Engine.
The Pump circulates the Coolant which cools the Engine.
It feeds the Motor through a Pipe.
The Motor powers the Pump and its Sensor monitors the Coolant.
The Controller regulates the Motor because the Engine requires steady Power.
They protect the System.
